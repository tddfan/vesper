System Methodology

I. The Macro Cycle

Expansion

Markets trending up, low volatility. Primary focus on Momentum strategies to capture the ride.

Peak

High optimism, prices overextended. Primary focus on Scalping / Profit Taking to lock in gains.

Contraction (High Fear: VIX > 25)

High fear regime detected. Secondary strategies like Volatility Defense & Mean Reversion are prioritized to protect capital while harvesting snap-back alpha.

Trough

Maximum pessimism. Primary focus on Aggressive Accumulation of high-quality laggards.

II. The 7-Strategy Dictionary

Strategy Layman's Explanation Signal Evidence
Momentum "The Trend is your Friend." Price consistently staying above its 50-day average.
Mean Reversion "Buying a deep dip for a snap-back." Triggered when a price is "stretched" too far down; RSI < 35 or 30-day perf < -10%.
Pairs Trading "The Relative Value play." If Tech is up but AI is down, we buy the AI "lagger" expecting it to catch up.
Volatility "Insurance during Storms." Triggered when the VIX (Fear Gauge) spikes. It moves money into Gold/Cash.
Scalping "Short-term volatility trading." Using high daily price swings (ATR) to lock in 1-2% gains within hours.
Arbitrage "The Market Mismatch." Spotting when a UK ETF is priced cheaper than the actual stocks it holds.
Hedging "Betting against the fall." Using Inverse ETFs or defensive assets to protect capital while sentiment is cold.
